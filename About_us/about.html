Our Vision

Our goal is to build a powerful and inclusive communication bridge between the Deaf community and the hearing world. Through our Sign Language Interpreter and Translator, we aim to make real-time communication easier, faster, and accessible to everyone.

Our Sign Language Interpreter uses advanced computer vision and AI models to understand hand gestures and convert them into clear, meaningful English text or speech. To go even further, our Sign Language Translator transforms this interpreted English into multiple languages, enabling seamless conversation across regions and cultures. By combining gesture recognition with multilingual translation, we strive to create a platform that breaks communication barriers, empowers individuals, and promotes equal participation for all.

Achievements

We built a real-time sign language interpreter and multilingual translator, enabling seamless communication between signers and non-signers. Our prototype showcases accurate gesture recognition and smooth language translation through a web-based interface.
